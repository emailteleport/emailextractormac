Welcome to the EmailExtractorMax documentation. Here you'll find comprehensive guides and documentation to help you start working with EmailExtractorMax as quickly as possible.

What is EmailExtractorMax?

EmailExtractorMax is a powerful email extraction tool designed exclusively for macOS. It helps you find and collect email addresses from multiple sources:

  • Search Engines - Extract emails from DuckDuckGo, Bing, Brave, and Yahoo search results
  • Local Files - Scan documents, spreadsheets, and other files on your computer
  • Email Clients - Access emails from Mac Mail and Outlook

Key Features

Multi-Source Extraction

Choose your extraction method based on your needs. Use search engines to discover new contacts, scan local files for existing data, or access your email client for established communications.

Smart Filtering

Filter your results with blacklists, whitelists, and pattern matching. Exclude unwanted domains and spam addresses to keep your lists clean and relevant.

Flexible Export

Export your extracted emails to TXT or CSV format. Customize column selection and delimiters to match your workflow and integrate with other tools.

Native macOS Performance

Built specifically for macOS, EmailExtractorMax runs smoothly on both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs, taking advantage of native system capabilities.

  • 100 MB free disk space
  • Internet connection (for search engine features)
