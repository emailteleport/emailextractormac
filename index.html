Powerful extraction from multiple sources

EmailExtractorMax combines multiple extraction methods into one powerful tool, giving you complete control over how you find and collect email addresses.

Search Engines

Extract emails from DuckDuckGo, Bing, Brave, and Yahoo. Run parallel searches across all engines simultaneously for maximum coverage.

Local Files

Scan folders and files for email addresses. Supports Word documents, Excel spreadsheets, text files, and many more formats.

Web Crawler

Crawl specific websites by URL with configurable depth. Scan entire domains and subdirectories for email addresses.

Mac Mail & Outlook

Extract emails directly from Apple Mail and Outlook. Scan headers, body, and filter by subject.

Smart Filtering

Blacklist unwanted domains, filter by patterns, exclude spam addresses. Keep only the emails that matter to you.

Flexible Export

Export to TXT or CSV with customizable delimiters. Choose which columns to include: email, source, search engine, and more.
