Powerful Features for Email Extraction

Everything you need to find and collect email addresses from multiple sources, all in one native macOS application.

Most Popular

Search Engine Extraction

Extract emails directly from search engine results. Our advanced crawler visits each result page and finds all email addresses.

  • Multiple engines: DuckDuckGo, Bing, Brave, Yahoo
  • Parallel search: Search all engines simultaneously
  • Deep crawling: Configurable depth and concurrent limits
  • Real-time results: See emails as they're found
  • Smart rate limiting: Avoid blocks automatically

Local File Scanning

Scan your local files and folders for email addresses. Perfect for extracting contacts from documents, spreadsheets, and archived emails.

  • Multiple formats: Word, Excel, PDF, TXT, and more
  • Folder scanning: Process entire directories recursively
  • Mail files: Support for .emlx (Apple Mail) files
  • Multi-threaded: Fast processing with parallel scanning

Email Client Integration

Extract email addresses directly from your email clients. Access your existing conversations and contacts without exporting manually.

  • Mac Mail: Direct access to your Apple Mail messages
  • Outlook: Scan Outlook profiles and folders
  • Subject filtering: Search by email subject
  • Headers & body: Extract from both parts
